Get the app See how it works

Up and running in minutes

No accounts to manage, no hardware to buy. If your students have phones, you have a field lab.

1

Create a class

Open Classroom Mode in the app and start a class. You get a short join code to share with your students.

2

Students join

Students enter the code to join. No emails required — they’re in the group instantly.

3

Assign a Field Trip Quest

Pick target species (with smart suggestions for your area), set how many to find, and optionally lock it to a park or trail by dropping a map pin.

4

Watch progress live

See each student’s tagged species and incidental finds roll in as they explore — for you and the whole class.

Field Trip Quests

Missions you design to match your lesson — a pond study, a schoolyard bird count, a fungi forage after the rain.

🎯

Target species

Choose the exact species you want students to find, with auto-suggestions drawn from what actually lives in your region.

📍

Region lock

Drop a pin on a park, trail, or campus and set a radius. Quests can require finds inside that boundary — perfect for a specific field-trip site.

📊

Live progress

Track target catches and incidental discoveries per student, visible to you and classmates to spark friendly momentum.

🧠

Real science

Every tag is a real, AI-identified observation with species, location, and conservation context — turning play into genuine ecology.
