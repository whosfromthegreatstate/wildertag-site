How It Works

Three steps. Real wildlife.

No lures, no bait, no fictional creatures. Just you, your phone, and the actual living world around you.

01 📷

Scan

Point your camera at any plant, animal, insect, or fungus. Our AI identifies the species in seconds.

02 🎯

Tag

Play the slingshot minigame to capture and tag the species. Nail the perfect shot for bonus XP and rare variants.

03 📖

Collect

Build your field journal. Track every species you encounter, earn mastery badges, and climb the leaderboards.

Features

Everything you need to explore.

Built for nature-curious people who want to learn what lives around them, not just scroll past it.

🧠

AI Species ID

Powered by computer vision trained on millions of species observations. Identifies plants, animals, insects, fungi, and more.

🗺️

Explore Map

See what species have been spotted nearby. Discover biodiversity hotspots, track seasonal migrations, and find new areas to explore.

👑

Royal Variants

Rare, golden-bordered species cards that appear at random. Collect them all and show off in the community leaderboards.

📆

Daily Challenges

New missions every day. Find a specific species, tag three insects before noon, or explore a biome you have never visited.

🎓

Species Mastery

Train on species you have tagged. Study their habitats, behaviors, and conservation status to earn mastery ranks.

🤝

Friends & Leagues

Add friends, compare field journals, join regional leagues, and compete on weekly leaderboards. Explore together or go head-to-head.

🌱

The Verdant Pledge

Every species you tag contributes to a living map of biodiversity. This data helps researchers track how ecosystems shift under climate change, in real time, at a resolution never before possible.

When the community hits conservation milestones, we donate directly to partner organizations working to protect the habitats your data helps document. Your tags have real impact.

Community Tags
Every observation strengthens the biodiversity dataset shared with researchers and conservation groups worldwide.
Milestone Donations
At every community milestone, funds go directly to conservation organizations protecting critical habitats.
Open Science
Aggregated, anonymized species data is shared freely with climate researchers tracking biodiversity shifts.
