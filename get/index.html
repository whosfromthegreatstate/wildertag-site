How it works

Three taps from "what is that?" to a track you made.

STEP 1
📸

Spot & identify

Point your camera at a plant, bird, insect, mammal, or mushroom — or listen for a bird's song. AI identifies it instantly.

STEP 2
🎛️

Remix its call

Drop into the Field Studio and turn a real bird call into a loop. Layer a beat, add a melody, and make it yours.

STEP 3
✨

Collect & share

Fill your field journal, level up, and share a video of your jam. Every find is a card worth showing off.
